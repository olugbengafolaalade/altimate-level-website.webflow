About Us

Founded in July 2021, we have worked with over 50 international applicants and counting. We aim to recruit, facilitate, and support foreign-educated registered nurses to qualify and work in the United States. Altimate Level believes that foreign-educated registered nurses will help suppress the shortage of healthcare workers in the United States.  Altimate Level seeks to supply skilled foreign educated nurses to US hospitals. Foreign educated registered nurses have proven more than capable of providing adequate care for all patients and Altimate Level provides the path for them to do so.

We support foreign-educated nurses aspiring to succeed in their careers

Our commitment goes beyond recruitment, we are dedicated to guiding and empowering foreign-educated registered nurses throughout every step of their journey.We recognize the unique aspirations and challenges faced by nurses seeking opportunities in the U.S. We provide not only recruitment services but also a comprehensive support system that ensures a smooth transition and a flourishing career path.

Our business values

Transparency

Our firm is committed to maintaining transparency in all transactions, recognizing the significance of openness with clients and employees for better client relationships.

Confidentiality

Our unique services are backed by a strict confidentiality policy, ensuring the protection of client information and data for the success of our business operations.

Integrity

Alimate Level Solutions prioritizes integrity, aligning with its vision to provide top-notch care and advance foreign-educated nurses' careers, adhering to moral and ethical guidelines for trust.

Integrations

Altimate Level Solutions promotes equality, diversity, and respect in the workforce, ensuring no unlawful discrimination based on age, disability, gender, religion, sex, or sexual orientation.

Quality

Altimate Level Solutions is a hands on firm. We pride ourselves in only providing the best of the best for our clients. We pay attention to details in order to stand out and yield quality nurses for our clients.

Our Mission

We promise to bridge the gap between international health care workers and the US health worker shortage. As well as providing highly qualified foreign-educated registered nurses with the pathway and resources to work in reputable US healthcare system and advance in their medical careers.

Our Vision

Our firm seeks to create a smooth pathway for foreign-educated registered nurses to achieve their United States Registered Nurse License. We facilitate all aspects of immigration, licensing, credentialing, testing, training, and education for candidates.
