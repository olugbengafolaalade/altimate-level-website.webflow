The Requirements

Currently a licensed registered general nursing, registered midwife or registered psychiatric nurse within your country of residence (subject to verification)

A graduate of a nurse training program from an accredited school of nursing with training in medical, surgical, obstetrics, pediatrics and psychiatry - both clinical and theory (BSN is preferred but not required).

At least one year of current and consistent post graduate experience at a healthcare facility with at least 100 beds in an acute care inpatient setting.

Meet the requirements as laid down by U.S. immigration for permanent residency applicants.

Meet English proficiency in accordance with Section 343 of the Illegal Immigration Reform and Immigration Responsibility Act of 1996 (VisaScreen).

Be willing to relocate to the U.S and flexible on assignment location.
